As you know, the Central Board of Direct Taxes (CBDT), via a circular clarified that employers will have to deduct TDS from salary for FY2021-22 as per the tax regime - new or old - chosen by the employee. If you want to go for the new tax regime, then you must inform us of this till Aug 27th, 2021 else by default TDS would be deducted as per old regime tax rates. For application format, you can refer to the attachment.
